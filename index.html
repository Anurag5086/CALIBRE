ABOUT CALIBRE - DTU

CALIBRE(Computational Analytic Learning and Intelligence Based REsearch) of DTU under “ Big Data Analytics and Web Intelligence ” Lab under the Computer Science and Engineering department of Delhi Technological University.

CALIBRE is a research group which mainly focuses on the following areas: Big Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Blockchain, IoT, Deep Learning, Online Social Networks Analysis, Recommender Systems, Knowledge Discovery, Computational Intelligence, Multimedia Technologies , Human Analytics, Web Engineering, Intelligent Systems,Data Science.

CALIBRE aims to promote research culture in students from an early stage hence it offers long term research projects and opportunities to interested and hardworking students.
